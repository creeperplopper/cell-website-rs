• What are cells?
  • Animal cells
  • Plant cells
  • Specialised animal cells

Any animal parts have many different components and systems. For example:​

  • Heart
  • Liver
  • Lungs
  • Intestines​
  • Circulatory system
  • Digestive system

To make these, there are different types of cells needed. And groups of cells with a common function are called tissues.​

The reason why cells look different is because they need to be different shapes to do their jobs. For example, the red blood cells has no nucleus to have more space for haemoglobin, the molecule that oxygen binds to.​ These are called specialised cells.
